Notes created Sun Dec 14 21:07:48 2003 by the Parts Tracker.

*** WARNING *** WARNING *** WARNING *** WANRING *** WARNING ***
Whilst every effort is made to maintain backward compatibility
of the LDraw parts library, there is one change in this parts 
release that could have a detrimental effect on existing models. 
If you do not update your own model files they will not render 
as they did prior to this update. 

The 9V Train wheel holder (2878.dat) was previously released
with the wheelset included. This was an error due to lack of 
clarity on how to handle composite parts.

The 2878.dat part in this update does NOT include the wheelset, 
but a new composite part 2878c01.dat is included. This DOES 
include the wheelset. The wheels are hardcoded in black allowing
easy modelling of the light grey wheel holders with black wheels.

To preserve the previous functionality you must replace any uses
of 2878.dat in you own models with 2878c01.dat.

Please accept the apologies of the LDraw team for this 
inconvenience.
